TYPHOONS

Typhoons inflict floods, significant damage to crops, residences, and other structures, as well as fatal accidents.
They also bring high winds and heavy rainfall.

ABOUT TYPHOONS

Introduction

There are various advantages to being aware of typhoon threats and its effects.
By making emergency plans, storing up on supplies, and taking the required precautions to reduce damage and preserve their safety, people can become more prepared for the storms.
Additonally, it may encourage more spending on research to better comprehend and forecast these storms as well as infrastructure that can withstand the effects of typhoons.
